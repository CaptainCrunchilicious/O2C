The following enhancements are planned to evolve the current O2C
implementation beyond its initial scope — leveraging modern SAP
capabilities and adjacent technologies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SAP FIORI DEPLOYMENT
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  What    : Replace the classical SAP GUI interface with SAP Fiori
            browser-based apps for O2C key functions.
  Apps    :
    - "Manage Sales Orders" — create and track orders from any device
    - "Track Deliveries" — real-time shipment status for field teams
    - "Create Billing Documents" — trigger invoicing without backend
  Benefit : Sales reps and managers can act on live data from mobile
            devices without requiring backend SAP access or training
            in the classic GUI.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. AI-POWERED DEMAND FORECASTING
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  What    : Integrate SAP Analytics Cloud with embedded machine learning
            models to predict future order volumes.
  Scope   :
    - Forecast by customer segment, product category, and region
    - Identify seasonal demand spikes before they hit inventory
    - Feed predictions into MRP to trigger proactive replenishment
  Benefit : Shift from reactive stock management to anticipatory planning
            — reducing stockouts, emergency orders, and excess inventory.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. AUTOMATED CREDIT LIMIT REVIEW
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  What    : Build a scheduled workflow that auto-reviews customer credit
            limits based on historical payment behaviour.
  Logic   :
    - Customers who pay consistently on time → credit limit auto-increase
    - Customers with repeated delays → limit freeze + manager alert
    - Monthly batch job triggers review without manual intervention
  Benefit : Credit risk managed dynamically, not just at onboarding.
            Reduces bad debt exposure and rewards reliable customers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. DYNAMIC PRICING ENGINE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  What    : Extend SAP's condition technique configuration with advanced
            pricing rules beyond standard list price.
  Features:
    - Time-based promotions (weekend pricing, quarter-end discounts)
    - Volume tier pricing (buy 100+ units → extra 5% off)
    - Customer-segment discounts (dealer vs. retail vs. project buyer)
    - Automatic price expiry and renewal triggers
  Benefit : Competitive pricing without manual reconfiguration every
            time the market or strategy changes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. REAL-TIME O2C KPI DASHBOARD
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  What    : Deploy a live analytics dashboard in SAP Analytics Cloud
            (or embedded in Fiori) to monitor O2C performance.
  KPIs Tracked:
    - Order-to-Invoice Cycle Time (in days)
    - Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)
    - On-Time Delivery Rate (%)
    - Invoice Accuracy Rate (%)
    - Revenue by Sales Organization / Division
    - Open AR Aging Report (30/60/90 day buckets)
  Benefit : Management gets real-time visibility without waiting for
            monthly reports. Issues are spotted and fixed in hours,
            not weeks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. SAP SALES CLOUD (CRM) INTEGRATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  What    : Connect the O2C process with SAP Sales Cloud (CRM) to
            create a seamless pipeline from lead acquisition to cash.
  Flow    :
    Opportunity won in CRM
    → Auto-trigger Quotation creation in SAP SD (VA21)
    → Customer accepts → Sales Order raised (VA01)
    → Order win updates CRM opportunity status automatically
    → Post-delivery → invoice PDF emailed via CRM to customer
  Benefit : Eliminates the gap between sales pipeline activity and ERP
            execution. Sales teams see live order status in CRM without
            switching systems.
